About the Company

AstraMind Health AI is a healthcare technology company focused on building secure, regulation-ready artificial intelligence systems for clinical, research, and population-scale medical applications.

Our mission is to enable precision care, accelerate medical breakthroughs, and ensure AI safety through responsible innovation.

Products & Solutions

  • AstraVision™ — AI for imaging diagnostics (Radiology + Pathology)
  • GeneFlow AI™ — Computational genomics & gene-editing assistance
  • ImmunoPredict™ — Immune response modeling for vaccine & therapy R&D
  • ClinGuard™ — Secure LLM for hospitals with bias-reduction layers
  • BioProtector™ — Healthcare data-security, anonymization & compliance suite
